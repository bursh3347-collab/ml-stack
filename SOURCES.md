## License Compliance Notes

- **MIT** (Ollama, LlamaIndex, DSPy, Langfuse core, LangChain, ONNX Runtime, W&B): Permissive, free commercial use
- **Apache-2.0** (HuggingFace, vLLM, TensorFlow, Haystack, MLflow, Ray, JAX): Permissive, patent grant
- **BSD-3-Clause** (PyTorch, Scikit-learn): Permissive, attribution required
- **CreativeML Open RAIL-M** (Stable Diffusion): Open with usage restrictions (no illegal content generation)
- **AGPL-3.0** (Open Interpreter): ⚠️ **Cannot use in proprietary SaaS.** Study patterns only.
- **MIT/EE** (Langfuse): Core is MIT, enterprise features under separate license
